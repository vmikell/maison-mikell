Maison

Support

For help with Maison, account access, household setup, subscriptions, or privacy requests, contact the Maison team at support@maisonhomeapp.com.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions purchased in the iOS app are managed through your Apple App Store account. Subscriptions purchased in the Android app are managed through your Google Play account.

Account Deletion

Maison includes an in-app account deletion path. If you cannot access the app, contact support from the email address tied to your Maison account.
