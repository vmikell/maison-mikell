Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Maison collects the account, household, planner, shopping, reminder, and diagnostic information needed to provide the app. Subscription purchases are processed by Apple, Google, and RevenueCat; Maison does not collect or store full payment card details.

Information We Use

We use sign-in details, household membership, invite codes, home profile answers, tasks, shopping items, completion history, reminder records, and app diagnostics to run and improve Maison.

Service Providers

Maison may use Firebase for authentication and data storage, RevenueCat for subscription status, Apple and Google for in-app purchases, and hosting or email providers needed to operate the service.

Your Choices

You can sign out, request account deletion from inside Maison, cancel subscriptions through your Apple App Store or Google Play account, and contact support for privacy questions.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact the Maison team at support@maisonmikell.com.
