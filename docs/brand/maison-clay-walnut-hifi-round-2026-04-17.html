Maison • Higher-Fidelity Round

Literaler product mockups for Clay Hearth and Richer Walnut

This round is meant to feel closer to actual Maison product surfaces, not just polished mood boards. The layouts are denser, the information hierarchy is more real, and the dashboards lean harder into what the app would actually need to show day to day.

You were right to call this out.The earlier rounds were real mockups, but still more conceptual than product-like. This pass makes Clay Hearth and Richer Walnut feel closer to the actual app so you and Riah can judge the colors in a more honest context.

Why these two

Clay Hearth has the most soul. Richer Walnut has the strongest bridge between the color system and the future type system.

What to judge here

Not just whether the colors are pretty, but whether the product still feels trustworthy, legible, and worth using every day.

What changes after seeing them more literally

Still beautiful, still soulful, but it needs more restraint to stay trustworthy as a real app. It wants to be a brand accent system more than a full-product blanket.

Richer Walnut

Feels stronger in real product context than it did in the looser mockups. It is the better bridge into the typography revisit because it already feels more structured.

My recommendation

If you and Riah want to sit with the colors, these are the two to sit with in real product terms: Clay Hearth for soul, Richer Walnut for branded polish.
