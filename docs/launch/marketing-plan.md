# Maison Marketing Plan

## Goal

Launch Maison to a focused first audience and prove that households outside Victor and Riah's own home will pay for it.

## Positioning

Do not market Maison as just another family organizer.

Lead with:
- a home operating system for couples
- shared home management without chaos
- maintenance, shopping, reminders, and planning in one calm app

## Ideal first customers

- couples sharing a home
- busy homeowners
- households who already feel coordination friction
- people who want calm, not hyper-optimized productivity culture

## 30-day launch plan

### Phase 1: Foundation
- finalize positioning
- finalize pricing
- create launch landing page
- create legal pages
- define app-store listing copy

### Phase 2: Asset creation
- create polished screenshots
- create short demo walkthrough
- create founder launch posts
- create direct message/email templates

### Phase 3: Controlled launch
- launch to warm network first
- invite a small number of outside households
- collect onboarding friction and objections
- improve the paywall and onboarding immediately based on real behavior

### Phase 4: Expansion
- push broader social/content outreach
- test couple/homeowner communities carefully
- highlight testimonials and real household outcomes
- close founding lifetime offer after 14 days

## Metrics to watch

- visitor to signup rate
- signup to household-created rate
- household-created to week-1 retained rate
- paid conversion rate
- yearly plan share
- refund/churn reasons

## Initial marketing principle

Do not try to reach everyone.

The first win is getting a small number of the right households to say:
"This actually makes our home life easier."

That proof matters more than broad, shallow reach.
