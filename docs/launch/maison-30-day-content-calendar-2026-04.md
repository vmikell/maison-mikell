# Maison 30-Day Content Calendar

Prepared: 2026-04-18

## Goal

Turn Maison's positioning into a real 30-day publishing system across Instagram, TikTok, and X.

This calendar is built to do three things:
- sharpen the message
- earn qualified visits and early-access signups
- identify which Maison angles actually pull in public

## Positioning guardrails

Keep Maison framed as:
- a calm shared-home system
- a product for people sharing a home life, not just couples-only language
- warm, premium, domestic, and specific

Avoid:
- generic productivity language
- startup hype
- over-explaining features before people care
- making every post sound like an ad

## Channel roles

### Instagram
Best for:
- trust
- taste
- saveable ideas
- shareable relationship / home-life clarity

### TikTok
Best for:
- discovery
- emotional recognition
- quick pattern validation
- finding which message gets immediate pull

### X
Best for:
- founder-led voice
- sharp observations
- reply loops
- message testing in public

## Weekly cadence

### Instagram
- 2 carousels
- 2 reels
- stories on publishing days

### TikTok
- 5 posts per week

### X
- 4 to 6 original posts per week
- replies on active days

## Core series to use this month

1. **Tiny home frictions**
   - the little recurring things that make shared-home life feel heavier than it should

2. **Calmer systems**
   - simple rituals, flows, and routines that reduce domestic chaos

3. **Before / after Maison**
   - contrast between fragmented home coordination and a shared system

4. **Founder notes**
   - honest, non-corporate observations about building Maison and what home life actually feels like

5. **Household hot takes**
   - short, pointed lines that invite replies and shares

# Asset Notes By Channel

## Instagram asset rules
- Carousels should be clean, warm, and saveable
- Reels should feel lived-in, not commercial
- Use domestic scenes, handwritten details, groceries, reminders, maintenance, and shared moments
- Do not make every Reel a screen recording

## TikTok asset rules
- Hook in the first 1 to 2 seconds
- Open with tension, not explanation
- Use real-life scenes whenever possible
- End with a simple next step, not a hard sell

## X asset rules
- Prefer sharp, human language
- Post from a founder point of view whenever possible
- Use replies as distribution, not just timeline broadcasting
- Keep links secondary when possible

# KPI Targets For The First 30 Days

## Instagram
- strong early signal: 0.5% to 0.9% engagement
- watch saves, shares, profile visits, and story taps

## TikTok
- strong early signal: 3% to 6% engagement by views
- watch average view quality, shares, comments, profile clicks

## X
- strong early signal: 0.03% to 0.10% engagement
- watch replies, profile visits, and conversation quality

## Conversion
- judge the first month mainly on:
  - landing-page visits
  - early-access / waitlist signups
  - recurring comments and objections
  - which series deserve repetition

# Operating Rule

Do not invent 30 unrelated posts.

Run a few strong Maison ideas repeatedly, measure honestly, and turn the winners into the real launch engine.
