## Full description, App Store / Play Store draft

Maison Mikell is a shared household app for couples and homes that want one calm place to stay on top of everyday life.

Instead of juggling notes, texts, reminders, and scattered lists, Maison Mikell helps bring the moving parts of a home together in one clear system.

Use Maison Mikell to:
- manage shared shopping lists
- track recurring home maintenance
- send and receive household reminders
- stay aligned on important household planning
- keep household coordination shared, not stuck in one person’s head

Maison Mikell is designed for real shared homes, not generic productivity culture. The experience should feel simple, calm, and useful enough to become part of everyday household rhythm.

## Why people use Maison Mikell
